Research Overview

These papers extend my research into generative models, vision-language models, and anomaly detection. The work includes human image generation with pose priors (AAAI 2025), counterfactual-aware procedural planning (ACM MM 2025), and visual-language models for anomaly detection (ACM MM 2025). Together, they reflect my broader contributions to advanced deep learning methods in computer vision and multimodal AI.
