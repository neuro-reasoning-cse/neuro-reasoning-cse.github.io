Medical Image Analysis

Meet the people working on it!

Research Overview

#Required My research focuses on pathology whole-slide images (WSIs) for cancer prognosis and related medical image analysis. The goal is to extract reliable and interpretable biomarkers from gigapixel pathology images to better predict cancer progression and patient outcomes.
